<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Unlock and Upgrade Classes">

Progression in Animal Hospital (Anomaly) is tied directly to your performance during the night shift. You earn two types of currency: Cash (for temporary items during the run) and Animal Coins (for permanent class unlocks). Understanding how to spend these effectively is the key to mastering the various **roblox animal hospital classes**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Survive the Night Shift">
    Complete your assigned tasks and keep your Sanity above zero. Each successful shift rewards you with Animal Coins based on the number of patients treated and anomalies correctly rejected.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit the Class Menu">
    In the main lobby, access the class selection screen. Here you can view the costs for new roles like the Surgeon (2,500 Coins) or the Psychologist (500 Coins).
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Invest in Upgrades">
    Once a class is purchased, don't stop there. Each class has three levels. Leveling up increases the potency of your perks, such as increasing the Sanity restored per heal or the number of Taser charges you start with.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Assign Your Main Role">
    Before starting a shift with a team, communicate which class you are using. Overlapping too many of the same class (like three Secretaries) is inefficient compared to a diverse team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
Don't buy every class immediately. Save your first 120 Coins for the Secretary, then leapfrog straight to the Doctor or Surgeon. This path provides the fastest return on your investment.
</Callout>

</Section>
