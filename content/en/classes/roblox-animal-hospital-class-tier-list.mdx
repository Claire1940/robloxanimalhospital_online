<Section icon={Shield} title="A-Tier: High Value & Reliability">

A-Tier classes are the backbone of most successful teams. They are affordable for mid-game players and provide consistent benefits that help mitigate the risks of the night shift.

**Doctor and Psychologist**
The **Doctor** (900 Coins) is the most reliable medical class for players who haven't yet saved enough for the Surgeon. It provides a steady stream of sanity recovery. The **Psychologist** (500 Coins) is a high-skill role that doubles all sanity effects. This means Coffee restores twice as much, but mistakes also hurt twice as bad.

**Secretary and Security**
The **Secretary** is the best early-game investment. Since most runs fail at the front desk, rewarding the receptionist with sanity for every correct check-in ensures the "gatekeeper" stays alive. **Security** is the best defensive option for F2P players, providing a Taser to stun threats without spending Robux.

| Feature | Doctor | Psychologist | Secretary | Security |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Best For** | Room 1-8 | Pro Players | Front Desk | Defense |
| **Sanity Help** | High (Healing) | Extreme (Variable) | Moderate (Check-in) | Low |
| **Utility** | Medical | Sanity Buffs | Reception | X-Taser |
| **Difficulty** | Easy | Hard | Easy | Medium |

<Callout type="success" title="Strategy Note">
Pair a **Secretary** at the front desk with a **Psychologist** in the hallway. The Psychologist can use the Secretary's successful check-ins to stay at 100% sanity while managing the more dangerous environmental hazards like the Bed Monster.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock and Upgrade Classes">

Progression in Animal Hospital (Anomaly) is tied to your ability to survive shifts and earn Animal Coins. Unlike the cash you spend in the shop, Animal Coins are permanent.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Survive the First Shift">
    Start as an **Intern**. Focus on learning the difference between normal patients and anomalies. Use the shutters at the front desk to reject suspicious visitors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Earn 120 Animal Coins">
    Your first goal should be the **Secretary**. This class makes the front desk role much safer and allows you to earn more coins by surviving longer shifts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Specialize Your Role">
    Decide if you prefer treatment or defense. If treatment, save 900 coins for the **Doctor**. If defense, save 1250 for **Security**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reach Level 3 Mastery">
    Once you own a class, continue using it to earn XP. Upgrading a class to Level 3 unlocks its most powerful passive traits, such as increased inventory or sanity regeneration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Class Leveling">
Each level increases the potency of the class perk. For example, a Level 1 Nurse has +1 inventory, but a Level 3 Nurse has +3. Always max out one class before buying a new one in the same tier.
</Callout>

</Section>
