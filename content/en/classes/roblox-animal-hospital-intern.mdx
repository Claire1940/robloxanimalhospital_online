<Section icon={ClipboardCheck} title="Core Gameplay Loop and Shift Survival">

As a **roblox animal hospital intern**, your shift is divided into two high-pressure phases: Reception and Treatment. You must verify that the animal entering the clinic is a legitimate patient and not a dangerous anomaly. Admitting an anomaly drains Sanity and may spawn a Skinwalker, which can actively hunt players and patients within the hospital corridors.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Reception Check">
    When a patient approaches the desk, perform a visual scan. Look for obvious deformities like extra eyes, realistic human-like teeth, or strange twitching. If anything looks "off," do not admit them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Photo and CCTV Verification">
    Compare the live patient to their provided photo. If the photo shows a different species or has "cursed" elements (like bleeding eyes), use the desk shutters immediately. Check the CCTV to see if the patient's shadow or reflection reveals a hidden form.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Medical Room Assignment">
    Once cleared, send the patient to an open room. Each room (1-8) has specific diagnostic requirements. Use the computer terminal to identify the illness and the required treatment item.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Emergency Response">
    Be prepared for random events like room fires or "Bed Monsters." Use the Fire Extinguisher or Taser as needed to protect the facility and your remaining Sanity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Sanity Risk">
Staring at a "cursed" photo for more than three seconds will rapidly drain your Sanity. Identify the anomaly and look away or close the shutters as fast as possible.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Eye} title="Anomaly Detection: Identifying the Threats">

The most critical skill for any **roblox animal hospital intern** is anomaly detection. The hospital is under constant threat from entities trying to infiltrate the ward. These anomalies often mimic normal animal patients but leave subtle clues that you must catch before they pass the front desk.

| Detection Method | Normal Sign | Anomaly Warning (Reject Immediately) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Visual Scan** | Two eyes, standard fur | **Three eyes, wide grins, twitching limbs** |
| **CCTV Monitor** | Normal movement | **Stretched limbs, staring at camera, black void body** |
| **Patient Photo** | Matches patient | **Cursed grin, bloodshot eyes, different species** |
| **Behavior** | Patient waits calmly | **Aggressive speech, sudden disappearing, floating** |
