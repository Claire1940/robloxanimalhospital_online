<Section icon={Swords} title="In-Depth Best Class Analysis">

### The Surgeon: The F2P Powerhouse
The **Surgeon** is widely considered the **roblox animal hospital best class** for players who do not wish to spend Robux. At Level 3, this class doubles the Sanity gain from healing patients. This creates a loop where the more you work, the safer you become. The added speed boost allows you to navigate between the surgery room and the pharmacy significantly faster than any other role.

### The Secret Agent: Premium Anomaly Control
For those willing to invest Robux, the **Secret Agent** provides a level of security that Animal Coins cannot buy. Starting with a gun allows you to neutralize Skinwalkers and hostile anomalies before they can drain your team's Sanity. As you level up this class, you even begin to regenerate ammo every round, making it the most sustainable combat role in the game.

### The Secretary: The Early Game Essential
Do not let the low cost of the **Secretary** fool you. For only 120 Animal Coins, you gain a role that recovers Sanity every time you correctly check in a patient. Since the front desk is where most anomalies are spotted, having a class that rewards this high-stress job with Sanity recovery is vital for early-game progression.
