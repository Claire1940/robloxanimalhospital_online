Surviving your first few nights in **roblox animal hospital** requires a strict adherence to hospital protocols. <Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Reception Check-In">
    Every round begins at the front desk. When a patient arrives, do not immediately admit them. You must perform a three-point check: visual inspection, CCTV verification, and photo comparison.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Identify and Reject Anomalies">
    If the patient displays any strange behavior or physical distortions, use the **Shutter Button** at the desk to reject them. Admitting an anomaly turns them into a Skinwalker, which will hunt you inside the hospital.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Treat Normal Patients">
    Once a patient is verified as normal, send them to their assigned room. Follow the on-screen prompts to diagnose them and apply the correct medical items like Herbs, Eye Drops, or Ointment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Sanity and Resources">
    Every interaction with an anomaly or a scary event drains your Sanity. Use Coffee or food items purchased from the shop to recover. If your Sanity hits zero, the shift ends in failure.
  </Step>
</Steps>
