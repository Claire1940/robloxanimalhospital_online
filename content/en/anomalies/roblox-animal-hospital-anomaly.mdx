<Section icon={Target} title="Anomaly Detection: How to Spot Mimics">

In **roblox animal hospital anomaly**, your survival depends on your ability to distinguish between a regular animal and a distorted mimic. Anomalies manifest in various ways, and the game provides three main tools for verification: direct visual inspection, CCTV monitors, and photo identification. As the shifts progress, these anomalies become more subtle, requiring players to memorize the "standard" look of every animal species in the game.

<Checklist
  id="anomaly-signs"
  title="Critical Anomaly Warning Signs:"
  items={[
    "Extra or missing limbs and eyes (e.g., a bunny with three eyes)",
    "Distorted facial features like wide, human-like grins or sharp teeth",
    "CCTV-only distortions where the body appears as a black void or shadow",
    "Mismatched photos where the patient at the desk doesn't match the ID",
    "Erratic behavior such as twitching, staring at cameras, or floating"

/>

When you identify an anomaly, you must immediately hit the shutter button. Failure to do so allows the entity to enter the hospital, where it may transform into a Skinwalker. Skinwalkers are hostile and will hunt players, requiring the use of a Taser or a Gun to neutralize.

| Detection Method | What to Look For | Severity |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Visual Check** | Sharp teeth, realistic eyes, twitching, extra limbs | High |
| **CCTV Monitor** | Black boxes over eyes, void bodies, limb stretching | Critical |
| **Photo ID** | Face parts differing from the live patient, cursed grain | High |
| **Behavioral** | Staring directly into the camera or the player's soul | Medium |

<Callout type="warning" title="CCTV Priority">
Some anomalies are invisible to the naked eye and only appear on the CCTV monitor. Always double-check the camera feed even if the patient looks perfectly normal at the desk.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Treatment Room Guide & Tasks">

Once a patient is admitted, they must be assigned to a room for treatment. In **roblox animal hospital anomaly**, different rooms require different mini-games. Completing these tasks accurately is the only way to earn the cash needed for shop items like Coffee.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Diagnosis">
    Interact with the computer in the treatment room to see what the patient needs. Check for symptoms like burns, broken bones, or internal parasites.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mini-Game Completion">
    Follow the on-screen prompts. For example, in Room 6 (X-Ray), you must follow a flashing square sequence. In Room 8 (Surgery), you must use specific tools like Scalpels in the correct order.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Item Application">
    Retrieve the required medicine (Herbs, Eye Drops, Ointment) from the hallway cabinets and apply them to the patient to finalize the cure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Room 8 Warning">
Surgery is a timed event. If you leave the room or take too long to select the next tool, the patient will die, resulting in a massive Sanity penalty for the whole team.
</Callout>

| Room Type | Mini-Game Mechanic | Required Items |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Rooms 1-5** | Basic Medical Analysis | Herbs, Eye Drops, Bandages |
| **Room 6** | X-Ray Sequence Memory | Diagnostic Tools, Ointment |
| **Room 7** | Heart Monitor Icon Clicking | Defibrillator, Medicine |
| **Room 8** | Multi-step Surgery | Scalpel, Scissors, Forceps |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Sanity and Item Management">

Sanity is your most valuable resource. In **roblox animal hospital anomaly**, your Sanity drains whenever you encounter scary events, look at cursed photos, or make mistakes. If it hits zero, your character dies. Managing this meter requires a combination of class perks and shop items.

**Common Sanity Drains:**
- Admitting an anomaly (Instant drop).
- Staring at "Wall Bangers" or ceiling threats.
- Failing a surgery mini-game.
- Engaging with Skinwalkers without a weapon.

<Callout type="warning" title="Coffee Conservation">
Do not drink Coffee when your Sanity is at 80%. Save your recovery items for the "Nightmare" phases where Sanity drains passively over time.
</Callout>

| Item | Use Case | Priority |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Coffee** | Restores a large chunk of Sanity | High |
| **Taser** | Stuns hostile anomalies/Skinwalkers | High (Late Shifts) |
| **Fire Extinguisher** | Puts out room fires | Medium |
| **Speed Cola** | Increases movement speed for 30s | Low |
| **Medkit** | Heals physical damage from attacks | Medium |

</Section>
