<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Anomaly Detection Guide">

To avoid **roblox animal hospital jumpscares**, you must follow a strict verification protocol for every patient that arrives at the front desk. Anomalies are often subtle, but they always leave clues that can be spotted if you are diligent.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Visual Inspection">
    Look directly at the patient. Check for physical distortions such as three eyes, hollow sockets, sharp "human-like" teeth, or a wide, unnatural grin. If the patient is twitching or has distorted limbs, it is an anomaly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Photo Comparison">
    Compare the patient's live appearance with their provided photo. Look for discrepancies in fur color, facial structure, or expression. Cursed photos—those with bloodshot eyes or static—are immediate grounds for rejection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="CCTV Verification">
    Switch to the lobby or check-in cameras. Some anomalies only reveal their true form on digital screens. Look for black boxes over eyes, void-like bodies, or limbs that appear much longer on the monitor than in person.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Final Decision">
    If any of the above checks fail, press the Shutter button immediately. This sends the anomaly away and prevents it from entering the hospital. If the patient is clear, accept them for treatment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Some anomalies can mimic a normal patient perfectly in person but will show a "Void" body on the CCTV. Never skip the camera check during later shifts.
</Callout>

| Detection Tool | What to Look For | Reliability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Direct Sight** | Twitching, extra limbs, realistic teeth | High for physical bugs |
| **CCTV Monitor** | Stretched limbs, void forms, camera staring | Essential for high-tier shifts |
| **Patient Photo** | Mismatched features, static, bloody eyes | 100% accurate for photo anomalies |
| **Behavior** | Moving while they should be still | Medium |

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Surviving Hostile Entities and Events">

When a mistake is made and an anomaly is admitted, the hospital environment becomes hostile. You will encounter various entities designed to trigger **roblox animal hospital jumpscares** and drain your sanity. Understanding these threats is the difference between a successful shift and a game over.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="The Skinwalker">
    The most dangerous threat. It originates from a missed anomaly and will actively hunt players. Use a **Taser** or **Gun** to neutralize it before it deals fatal damage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="The Bed Monster">
    A stationary threat located under treatment beds. If you hear growling or see the bed shaking, do not stand near it. It will trigger a loud jumpscare if you linger too long.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Environmental Hazards">
    Includes green slime on the floor and wall-based anomalies. These usually drain sanity rather than killing you instantly, but they make you more vulnerable to other scares.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ritual Events">
    Occasional events like the Death Ritual require players to clear candles. Failing to do so results in a massive sanity drop and a screen-wide horror effect.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
