<Section icon={Map} title="Photo Verification and Identity Checks">

Documentation is your strongest weapon. Every patient arrives with a photo that represents their "Normal" state. If there is any discrepancy between the photo and the live patient, you are dealing with an entry from the **roblox animal hospital anomalies list**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Photo Scan">
    Check the photo provided at the desk. Look for "Cursed" effects like bloodshot eyes, static, or grainy filters that indicate the photo itself is an anomaly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Side-by-Side Comparison">
    Look at the live patient, then the photo. Focus on the ears, nose shape, and eye color. If the photo shows a cat with blue eyes but the patient has yellow eyes, reject them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Identity Confirmation">
    Some anomalies attempt to mimic the photo perfectly but fail on small details like height or the presence of specific markings (spots/stripes).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Sanity Note">
