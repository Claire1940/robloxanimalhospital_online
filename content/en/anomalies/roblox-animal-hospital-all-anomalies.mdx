<Section icon={Shield} title="Environmental Threats and Hostile Entities">

Beyond the patients at the desk, **roblox animal hospital all anomalies** include environmental hazards that can drain your sanity or kill you instantly. These threats do not follow the standard check-in rules and require specific items or actions to neutralize.

The Bed Monster and Wall Bangers are the most frequent environmental threats. The Bed Monster hides under treatment tables and will attack if you stay in the room too long without completing the task. Wall Bangers appear in the hallways; you must avoid looking at them directly to prevent massive sanity loss.

| Threat Name | Location | Counter Action | Sanity Impact |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Sanity Management and Survival Steps">

In **roblox animal hospital all anomalies**, your sanity is your health bar. Once it hits zero, you are eliminated from the shift. Managing this meter is just as important as treating the animals. Sanity drops when you encounter anomalies, stare at cursed images, or allow enemies to attack you.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor the Meter">
    Always keep an eye on the sanity bar at the bottom of your screen. If it starts shaking, you are taking passive damage from a nearby threat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Use Recovery Items">
    Coffee is your best friend. Drink it when your sanity drops below 50%. Shop food can also provide minor boosts between tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Rotate Duties">
    If you are the reception player, your sanity will drop faster due to constant anomaly checks. Swap roles with a teammate to recover.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Avoid Cursed Clues">
    Do not stare at "Cursed Photos" for longer than a second. Once you identify the anomaly, look away and hit the shutter button.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Sanity recovery items can be purchased in the shop using cash earned during the current shift. Unlike Animal Coins, this cash does not persist between games, so spend it liberally to ensure your survival.

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategic Recovery">
Wait until the "Emergency" events (like fires or rituals) are over before using your high-tier sanity items, as these events often drain sanity in chunks.
</Callout>
