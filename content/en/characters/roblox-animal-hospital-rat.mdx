<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Rat Verification Protocol">

Succeeding in [Animal Hospital (Anomaly)](https://www.roblox.com/games/78515283254292/Animal-Hospital) requires a methodical approach to reception duty. Follow this exact verification sequence whenever a rat patient approaches your front desk.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Visual Inspection">
    Observe the rat patient through the reception window. Look closely at its face, ears, and body. Check if it has a wide, unnatural grin, extra eyes, or if its body is constantly twitching.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="CCTV Camera Verification">
    Switch your monitor to the lobby or check-in camera feed. Some anomalies are invisible to the naked eye but show up on screen as completely black silhouettes or distorted, stretched figures.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Photo ID Comparison">
    Compare the paper photo on your desk with the patient standing before you. Look for subtle differences in ear shape, nose structure, or cursed visual effects like static and bloodshot eyes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Make Your Decision">
    If the rat passes all checks, accept them for treatment. If you spot even a single discrepancy, immediately press the shutter button to reject them and send the anomaly away.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
If a rat patient looks normal but your instincts tell you something is wrong, always double-check the CCTV feed. The camera monitor never lies.
