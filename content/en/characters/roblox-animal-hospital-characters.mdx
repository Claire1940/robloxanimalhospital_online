To consistently clear high-difficulty shifts, your team must distribute roles efficiently. Combining the strengths of different **roblox animal hospital characters** ensures all areas of the clinic remain secure.

### The 3-Player Efficiency Setup
This balanced composition is highly recommended for standard public and private lobbies looking for reliable clears.

- **The Receptionist (Secretary)**: Stays at the front desk permanently. They handle all check-ins, identify anomalies, and maintain high Sanity through their passive class perks.
- **The Medic (Surgeon or Doctor)**: Manages rooms 1 through 8. They execute diagnostic tasks, carry medical supplies, and recover Sanity rapidly with every successful treatment.
- **The Defender (Security or Paramedic)**: Clears environmental hazards, manages room fires with extinguishers, and uses Tasers to control any missed anomalies that breach the lobby.

### The Solo Survival Setup
If you are playing matchmaking alone, your priority is self-reliance.

- **Primary Choice**: **Surgeon**. The combination of self-healing Sanity and movement speed allows you to run between the reception desk and treatment rooms without falling behind.
- **Budget Alternative**: **Psychologist**. If you have excellent visual memory and rarely make check-in mistakes, the doubled positive Sanity effects will keep you healthy throughout the entire shift.
