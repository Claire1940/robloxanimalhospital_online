<Section icon={Target} title="How to Spot and Reject Anomalies">

Anomaly detection is the most critical skill required to survive your shifts. Anomalies are hostile entities disguised as normal animal patients. If you accept them, they transform into Skinwalkers that haunt the halls, drain your Sanity, and attack players.

### Visual, CCTV, and Photo Checks

To keep the hospital safe, you must utilize three distinct layers of inspection before opening the shutters to admit any visitor.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Visual Signs">
    Look for physical deformities on the patient at the desk. Watch out for **three eyes**, completely hollow sockets, sharp realistic teeth, wide grins, or unnatural twitching.
  </Card>
  <Card title="CCTV Signs">
    Check the lobby and check-in cameras. Look for black boxes over the eyes, stretched limbs, or a completely black void-like body that only appears on screen.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Photo Signs">
    Compare the live patient with their official photo. Reject them immediately if the photo has bloodshot eyes, a creepy grin, or does not match the animal's species.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Sanity Management and Survival Items">

Sanity acts as your health bar in **roblox animal hospital oggy**. If your Sanity hits zero, your run ends immediately. Managing this resource is just as important as treating your patients.

### Sanity Drain Causes and Recovery

Sanity drops when you make mistakes, such as admitting anomalies, taking damage from entities, or interacting with cursed environmental events like the Death Ritual. To recover, you must use shop items purchased with cash earned during the shift.

| Item | Category | Use Case | Priority | Mistake to Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Coffee** | Sanity | Restores Sanity during long shifts | High | Using it too early |
| **Taser** | Threat Control | Stops active Skinwalkers in hallways | High (Late Shifts) | Wasting charges on non-threats |
| **Fire Extinguisher** | Emergency | Clears fires and burning patients | Medium | Ignoring follow-up ointments |
| **Gun** | Threat Control | Ranged defense against hostile entities | Team-dependent | Firing near normal patients |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor Your Meter">
    Keep a close eye on your Sanity level. If it drops below 50%, prepare to swap roles with a teammate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Coffee and Food">
    Keep a steady supply of Coffee in your inventory. Use it immediately after handling rituals or surviving a Skinwalker encounter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assign Safe Tasks">

</Steps>
