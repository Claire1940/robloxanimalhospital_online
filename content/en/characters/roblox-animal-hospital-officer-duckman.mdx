<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Shift Survival Guide">

Successfully completing a night shift in **roblox animal hospital** requires a disciplined approach. Follow this structured protocol to keep your clinic safe and efficient.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Front Desk Reception">
    When the shift begins, at least one player must man the reception desk. When a patient arrives, do not immediately open the gates. Retrieve their file and prepare for visual inspection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Conduct the Triple-Check Inspection">
    Compare the physical patient at the desk against their file photo and the live CCTV monitor. Look for discrepancies in eyes, teeth, limb proportions, or overall behavior.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Process or Reject the Visitor">
    If the patient matches all documentation, accept them into the clinic. If any anomaly is detected, immediately pull the shutter switch at the desk to reject them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Target Treatments">
    Guide accepted patients to designated treatment rooms (Rooms 1-8). Complete the specific room tasks (such as X-Ray sequences or Heart Monitor clicks) and administer the exact items needed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Manage Clinic Sanity and Hazards">
    Keep Sanity high by drinking Coffee. If environmental threats like Bed Monsters or Wall Bangers appear, deploy counter-items like Tasers or clear hazards immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
Giving the wrong treatment item to a normal patient will kill them instantly. This severely penalizes your shift rating and drains team Sanity.
</Callout>
