Sanity is a depleting resource that drops whenever you encounter something "wrong." ### The Best Classes for Sanity Management

- **Surgeon (S-Tier)**: This is the best free-to-play class. It gains Sanity and a speed boost every time you successfully heal a patient. At Level 3, the Sanity gain is doubled, often removing the need for coffee entirely if you are fast at treatments.
- **Psychologist (A-Tier)**: A specialized class that doubles all Sanity effects. This means coffee restores twice as much, but mistakes also hurt twice as much. This is for expert players who rarely miss an anomaly.
- **Secretary (A-Tier)**: The perfect class for the front desk. You recover Sanity just by checking in patients correctly. If you are the **roblox animal hospital coffee guy** at the reception, this class is a must-buy for 120 Animal Coins.
- **Intern (C-Tier)**: The starter class. It provides a flat bonus to your maximum Sanity (up to +20 at Level 3), making it a safe choice for beginners learning the mechanics.

### Treatment Room Sanity Risks
- **Rooms 1-5**: Standard treatments. Low risk unless a fire breaks out.
- **Room 6 (X-Ray)**: Medium risk. Failing the flashing square sequence causes a sanity drop.
- **Room 7 (Heart Monitor)**: High risk. Clicking the skull icons drains sanity and slows down the treatment.
- **Room 8 (Surgery)**: Critical risk. This room requires specific tools (Scalpel, Scissors). Leaving mid-surgery kills the patient and causes a massive sanity shock to the player.
