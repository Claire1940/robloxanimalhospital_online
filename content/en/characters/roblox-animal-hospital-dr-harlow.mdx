<Section icon={Shield} title="Lore and the Animal Corporation Mystery">

Once you move past the initial tutorial shifts, **roblox animal hospital dr harlow** becomes a source of deep environmental storytelling. By interacting with him in the hospital lobby after Shift 7, players can piece together the dark history of the hospital and the corporation that funds it.

Dr. Harlow often speaks in a wistful, tired manner. He reveals that he has been with the hospital for a very long time and has witnessed countless employees come and go. His loyalty to the "Animal Corporation" seems to stem from a life-saving event in his past, though he remains vague about the details.

**Key Lore Observations:**
- **The Research**: Harlow believes that once "The Research" is finished, the anomalies and long shifts will finally end.
- **The Secret Box**: Players have spotted Dr. Harlow meeting with a hazmat-suited employee outside the hospital. He receives a box labeled **CONFIDENTIAL** and returns inside.
- **Trauma**: He hints at an unspecified event that the Animal Corporation saved him from, suggesting he may be working off a debt.

| Interaction Type | Frequency | Outcome |

| **Lobby Dialogue** | Once per shift | Reveals backstory and world-building |

| **Shop Visit** | Occasional | Small flavor text interactions |

<Callout type="info" title="Hidden Interaction">
