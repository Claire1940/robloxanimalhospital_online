<Section icon={Target} title="The Core Gameplay Loop: How to Play">

To succeed in **roblox animal hospital barney**, you must follow a strict protocol. The game rewards methodical players who utilize every tool available, from the CCTV monitors to the printed patient photos. If a patient looks "off"—perhaps their eyes are too realistic or their jaw is unhinged—they are likely an anomaly that must be rejected immediately.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reception & Screening">
    When a patient arrives at the front desk, do not click 'Accept' immediately. Look at their physical features. Check for "Barney-style" distortions, glowing eyes, or extra limbs. Use the CCTV button to view them from a different angle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Photo Verification">
    Compare the live patient to the photo on your desk. If the photo shows a different species, a distorted face, or a "cursed" bloodshot effect, use the shutter button to reject them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Treatment & Medicine">
    Once a patient is admitted, they move to a room. Use the computer to diagnose them. Match the required item (Herbs, Eye Drops, etc.) to the patient. Using the wrong item will kill the patient.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Sanity Recovery">
    As the shift progresses, your Sanity will drop. Use Coffee or food from the shop to restore it. Avoid looking at wall anomalies or "Bed Monsters" for too long, as these accelerate Sanity loss.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Tip">
If you are playing with a team, assign one person as the "Runner" who carries Coffee and Tasers to players in need. This prevents the Receptionist from having to leave their post.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Heart} title="Sanity Management & Survival Items">

Sanity is your health bar in **roblox animal hospital barney**. If it hits zero, your character dies, and the shift ends for you. Many events in the hospital, such as the "Death Ritual" or encountering a "Wall Banger," are designed to drain this meter rapidly. You must manage your resources to keep your mind intact.

| Item | Category | Effect | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Coffee** | Sanity | Restores moderate Sanity | Save for late-shift rituals |
| **Taser** | Defense | Stuns hostile anomalies | Use only on Skinwalkers |
| **Fire Extinguisher** | Utility | Clears room fires | Keep one in the hallway |
| **Speed Cola** | Utility | Increases move speed | Great for Room 8 (Surgery) |
| **Medical Herbs** | Treatment | Heals basic patients | Keep stock in inventory |

**How to Prevent Sanity Loss:**
1. **Avoid the Gaze**: Do not stare at cursed photos or wall anomalies.
2. **Correct Rejections**: Successfully rejecting an anomaly provides a small Sanity buffer.
3. **Team Support**: Stay near teammates who have Sanity-restoring perks.
4. **Room Efficiency**: Finish treatment tasks (like the Heart Monitor in Room 7) quickly to minimize exposure to hostile environments.

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
The "Bed Monster" is a common trap. If you see eyes under a patient's bed, do not interact with the patient until the eyes disappear, or your Sanity will plummet.
</Callout>

As the shifts progress in **roblox animal hospital barney**, the complexity of patient care increases. While Rooms 1-5 are straightforward, the later rooms require specific mini-game mastery. **Room-Specific Strategies:**
- **Room 6 (X-Ray)**: Pay close attention to the flashing square sequence. If you miss a step, do not guess; wait for the sequence to restart to avoid a fatal error.
- **Room 7 (Heart Monitor)**: Click the icons as they appear but **strictly avoid the skulls**. Clicking a skull resets your progress and triggers a jump-scare.
- **Room 8 (Surgery)**: This is the highest-stakes room. You must use tools like Scissors and Scalpels in the exact order shown on the screen. This room is best handled by a player with the **Surgeon** class for the speed boost.
