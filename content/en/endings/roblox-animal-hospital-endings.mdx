<Section icon={Target} title="How to Spot Anomalies and Prevent Bad Endings">

Preventing failure endings in Roblox Animal Hospital comes down to your ability to spot anomalies at the front desk. When a patient arrives, you must run a full diagnostic check before pressing the green accept button. If any suspicious traits are present, immediately pull the shutter switch to reject them.

| Checkpoint | Normal State | Anomaly Signs (Reject Immediately) |
|---|---|---|
| **In-Person View** | Normal eyes, standard animal teeth, calm behavior | Three eyes, hollow sockets, sharp human teeth, wide grins, constant twitching |
| **CCTV Monitor** | Clear image matching the desk view | Black box over eyes, stretched limbs, void-like body, staring directly at camera |
| **Photo ID** | Clear photo matching the patient | Grainy static, bloodshot eyes, distorted facial features, mismatched animal type |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Visual Inspection">
    Look closely at the patient at the reception desk. Check their eyes, mouth, and overall body shape for any unnatural movements or anatomical distortions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Camera Verification">
    Verify the patient on the lobby and check-in cameras. Some anomalies only reveal their true, distorted forms on the digital CCTV monitor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Photo ID Comparison">
    Compare the patient's physical appearance with their registered photo ID. Look out for cursed visual effects like static or bloodshot eyes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Make Your Decision">
    If the animal is normal, accept them for treatment. If any anomaly is detected, pull the shutters to send them away safely.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Establish a Routine">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Sanity Management and Survival Tactics">

Your Sanity level is the ultimate survival meter in Roblox Animal Hospital. Making mistakes, staring at cursed objects, or letting anomalies slip by will rapidly drain your Sanity. If your Sanity hits zero, your character dies, bringing an abrupt and tragic ending to your run.

To keep your Sanity high, you must utilize shop items, manage environmental hazards, and coordinate roles with your teammates.
