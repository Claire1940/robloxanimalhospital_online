The most critical barrier to reaching the **roblox animal hospital ending** is the presence of anomalies. These entities mimic regular animal patients but possess subtle, terrifying differences. If you admit an anomaly, it will eventually transform or attack, draining your sanity and potentially killing you before the shift ends. 

You must utilize three primary tools to verify every visitor:
1. **In-Person Inspection**: Look for physical deformities like extra eyes or realistic human teeth.
2. **CCTV Feed**: Some anomalies only reveal their true form on camera, showing distorted limbs or blacked-out eyes.
3. **Photo Comparison**: The patient's provided photo must match their current appearance exactly.

| Anomaly Type | Detection Method | Visual Clue | Action Required |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Sanity and Item Management Guide">

Your sanity meter is effectively your health bar. If it hits zero, your journey toward the **roblox animal hospital ending** stops immediately. Sanity drops when you make mistakes, encounter hostile events, or stare at anomalies for too long. Managing this resource requires a combination of class perks and shop items purchased with cash earned during the shift.

**Essential Survival Items:**
- **Coffee**: The primary tool for sanity recovery. Save it for the final hours of the shift.
- **Taser/Gun**: Used to neutralize anomalies that have already entered the hospital.
- **Fire Extinguisher**: Necessary for room fires and burning patient events.
- **Medical Supplies**: Herbs, eye drops, and ointments required for specific patient treatments.

| Item | Category | Priority | Usage Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Coffee** | Recovery | High | Use when sanity falls below 30% |
| **X-Taser** | Defense | Medium | Save for Skinwalker emergencies |
| **Ointment** | Treatment | Low | Only for burning patient events |
| **Surgery Tools** | Treatment | Critical | Must be used in Room 8 immediately |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Surviving a Night Shift">

To reach the **roblox animal hospital ending** consistently, you need a disciplined routine. The pressure increases as the clock ticks toward 6:00 AM, and a single mismanaged patient can lead to a cascade of failures. Follow this professional workflow to maximize your survival rate.

| Time Block | Focus Area | Action Plan |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **10 PM - 12 AM** | Efficiency | Process patients quickly to build up a cash reserve. |
| **12 AM - 2 AM** | Observation | Start checking CCTV for every patient; anomalies become more common. |
| **2 AM - 4 AM** | Survival | Handle environmental threats like Bed Monsters and Harlow events. |
| **4 AM - 6 AM** | Crisis Management | Use Coffee and weapons as needed; ignore non-essential tasks. |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Reception Filter">
    Every patient must pass the visual, CCTV, and photo check. If any check fails, hit the shutter button. Never admit a patient "just to be safe."
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Room Assignment">
    Send normal patients to their designated rooms (1-8). Ensure you have the correct treatment items ready before entering the room.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Treatment Execution">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Emergency Response">
    If a Skinwalker appears or a fire starts, drop your current task and address the threat immediately. Sanity preservation takes priority over patient care.
  </Step>
</Steps>
