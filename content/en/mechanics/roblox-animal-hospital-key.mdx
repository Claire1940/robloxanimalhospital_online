<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Shift Survival Guide">

Completing a night shift requires systematic execution. Follow this structured walkthrough to safely guide your team from clock-in to clock-out.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Reception Desk">
    At the start of the shift, at least one player must occupy the front desk. This player acts as the first line of defense, controlling the shutters to block suspicious entities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Run the Triple-Check Verification">
    When a patient arrives, perform a visual inspection, check the CCTV feed for hidden distortions, and compare the patient's physical appearance with their official file photo.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Process the Visitor">
    If the patient is normal, accept them into the clinic. If any anomaly is detected, immediately hit the shutter button to reject them and send them away.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Administer Correct Treatments">
    Guide accepted patients to designated treatment rooms. Complete the room-specific mini-games (such as X-Ray sequences or Heart Monitor clicks) and apply the correct medical items.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Manage Environmental Hazards">
    Keep an eye out for random events like fires, green floor slime, Bed Monsters, or Wall Bangers. Clear them quickly using the appropriate tools before they drain your Sanity.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
If you are playing solo, prioritize the reception desk. Let incoming normal patients wait in the lobby briefly while you ensure no anomalies slip through the front doors.

Anomalies are mutated or supernatural entities masquerading as regular animal patients. Spotting them requires keen observation. Use this checklist to identify anomalous features across three distinct check categories.

<Checklist
  id="anomaly-detection-checklist"
  title="Anomaly Detection Signs:"
  items={[

    "CCTV Monitor: Watch for black boxes covering eyes, stretched limbs, completely black silhouettes, or different facial structures.",
    "Photo Files: Check for static effects, mismatched species photos, or bloody/distorted expressions on the ID card.",
    "Environmental: Listen for aggressive scratching, deep growling, or sudden lights flickering near the check-in desk."

When any of these signs are present, do not press the accept button. Use the shutter controls to lock the entrance and force the entity to leave.

| Anomaly Category | Common Visual Clues | Immediate Counteraction |
|---|---|---|
| **Physical Mutation** | Extra eyes, human teeth, distorted limbs | Close shutters immediately |
| **Camera Distortion** | Static, pitch-black body, staring at camera | Verify via CCTV, reject visitor |
| **Cursed Photo** | Bloodshot eyes, grainy photo, wrong name | Reject visitor, avoid staring at photo |

<Callout type="danger" title="Sanity Risk">
Staring at cursed photos or hostile entities for too long causes rapid Sanity loss. Close the shutters or look away as soon as you confirm the threat.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Activity} title="Sanity, Items, and Medical Treatments">

Sanity is your actual health bar. If your Sanity drops to zero, you die, ending your run. Wrong diagnoses, missed anomalies, and environmental hazards all drain your Sanity. Use items purchased from the shop with shift cash to keep your team stable.

| Item Name | Category | Primary Use Case | Common Mistake to Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Coffee** | Sanity Recovery | Restores Sanity during long shifts | Wasting it early before high-threat events |
| **X-Taser** | Defense | Stuns aggressive Skinwalkers | Firing too early and missing the shot |
| **Fire Extinguisher** | Utility | Puts out room fires and burning patients | Ignoring the patient's medical follow-up |
| **Ointment** | Treatment | Heals burn victims after fires are cleared | Applying before putting out the fire |
| **Herbs / Drops** | Treatment | Basic medical supplies for Rooms 1-5 | Overstocking before checking room requirements |

Different treatment rooms require unique mini-games to successfully heal patients. Make sure you understand the mechanics of each room before interacting with the medical equipment.

| Room Number | Treatment Type | Core Mechanic | Failure Penalty |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Rooms 1-5** | Basic Medical | Analyze via computer, apply listed items | Patient dies, Sanity loss |
| **Room 6** | X-Ray | Complete the flashing-square sequence | Must restart sequence, wastes time |
| **Room 7** | Heart Monitor | Click matching icons, avoid clicking skulls | Slows down task progress |
| **Room 8** | Surgery | Use Scissors and Scalpels in shown order | Instant patient death |
