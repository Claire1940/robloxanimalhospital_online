<Section icon={Target} title="How to Play: Step-by-Step Shift Walkthrough">

Succeeding in your first few shifts requires a disciplined routine. The game loops through a series of increasingly difficult nights where speed and accuracy are equally vital.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Front Desk">
    Your shift begins at the reception area. As patients approach, interact with them to open their files. Do not unlock the lobby doors until you have thoroughly examined their appearance, photo ID, and CCTV feed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Run the Three-Point Anomaly Check">
    Compare the live animal standing in front of you with their official photo. Check the surveillance cameras for hidden distortions. If everything matches the standard profile, click the button to admit them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reject Anomalies Instantly">
    If you spot a wide grin, extra limbs, or a corrupted ID photo, pull the shutter switch immediately. This safely rejects the entity and prevents it from infiltrating your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Medical Treatments">
    Admitted patients go to designated rooms (Rooms 1-8). Check the room's computer terminal, complete the diagnostic mini-game, and retrieve the matching medicine from the hallway shelves.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Manage Your Sanity and Supplies">
    Keep your Sanity level above zero. If you encounter terrifying events, consume Coffee or food from the shop. Use your earned shift cash to buy defensive tools like Tasers for emergencies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Role Delegation">
If playing in a group, assign one player permanently to reception and camera duty. Having a dedicated gatekeeper drastically reduces the chance of admitting an anomaly.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Anomaly Detection & Warning Signs Checklist">

Spotting anomalies is your primary line of defense in **Roblox Animal Hospital**. Anomalies present themselves in three distinct ways: in-person physical deformities, camera distortions, and corrupted paperwork.

| Anomaly Category | Common Signs & Clues | Required Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **In-Person Visuals** | Wide human grins, hollow eyes, twitching, realistic teeth, three eyes | Reject immediately via shutters |
| **CCTV Feed** | Black boxes over eyes, completely black silhouettes, distorted limbs | Reject immediately via shutters |
| **Photo ID Cards** | Cursed grins, bloodshot eyes, static/grainy filters, mismatched species | Reject immediately via shutters |
| **Room Hazards** | Slime on floors, Bed Monsters, Wall Bangers, ceiling entities | Use special tools (Tasers, Extinguishers) |

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Visual Anomalies">
    Look closely at the face. Normal Roblox animals have simple, cute designs. If you see highly detailed human eyes or sharp, realistic teeth, it is an anomaly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Camera Anomalies">
    Some entities look perfectly normal to the naked eye but reveal their true, distorted form on the lobby security cameras. Always cross-reference the live view with the CCTV monitor.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Photo Anomalies">
    Always compare the patient's face with their paperwork. If the photo has a static overlay, red eyes, or does not match the animal's physical features, deny entry.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
