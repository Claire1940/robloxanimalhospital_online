<Section icon={Shield} title="How to Identify and Reject Anomalies">

Spotting anomalies before they cross the threshold is your primary defense against the **roblox animal hospital stalker** threats. The game provides multiple tools at the reception desk to help you analyze each visitor. You must compare the physical patient standing in front of you with their official photo and the live CCTV feeds.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Visual Anomalies">
    Look for physical deformities like **three eyes**, hollow sockets, sharp human-like teeth, or an unnaturally wide grin.
  </Card>
  <Card title="CCTV Tells">
    Check the monitor for black boxes covering the patient's eyes, distorted limbs, or a completely black, void-like silhouette.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Photo Discrepancies">
    Reject patients immediately if their live appearance does not match their paperwork photo, or if the photo has a bloodshot, cursed filter.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

| Anomaly Type | Key Warning Sign | Required Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Physical Deformity** | Sharp teeth, extra limbs, glowing eyes, or twitching. | Pull the shutter button immediately. |
| **CCTV Glitch** | Patient stares directly into camera with distorted body. | Reject via desk controls. |
| **Cursed Photo** | Static grain or bloody facial features on files. | Close reception shutters. |
| **Bed Monster** | Strange growling or movement under treatment beds. | Avoid checking under the bed. |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Develop a strict inspection routine for every single visitor: first check their face visually, then look at the photo document, and finally glance at the CCTV monitor. If even one detail feels off, reject them.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Night Shift Walkthrough">

Surviving a full night shift requires coordination and speed. Follow this optimized protocol to keep the hospital running smoothly while keeping stalkers at bay.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Reception Desk">
    At the start of the shift, assign at least one player to the front desk. This player is solely responsible for checking in normal animal patients and shutting out anomalies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Route Patients to Treatment Rooms">
    Once a patient is verified as safe, guide them to an open treatment room (Rooms 1-8). Each room features specific diagnostic equipment and requires unique medical mini-games.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Correct Treatments">
    Interact with the room computer to identify the required medicine or surgical tools. Delivering the wrong treatment kills the patient and severely penalizes your shift rating.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Environmental Hazards">
    Watch out for random events like room fires, wall bangers near reception, or green slime on the floor. Use fire extinguishers and keep your distance from hostile wall anomalies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Treatment Room | Gameplay Mechanic | Common Mistake to Avoid |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rooms 1-5** | Analyze computer, fetch matching shelf items. | Grabbing the wrong medicine. |
| **Room 6 (X-Ray)** | Complete the flashing-square sequence. | Guessing the final treatment item. |
| **Room 7 (Heart)** | Click matching icons while avoiding skull icons. | Panic-clicking skulls under pressure. |
| **Room 8 (Surgery)** | Use timed tools like scalpels and scissors. | Leaving the room mid-operation. |
