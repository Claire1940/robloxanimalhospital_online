<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Clearing the Ritual Candles">

To stop the **roblox animal hospital death ritual**, players must locate and interact with specific objects spawned by the event—most commonly ritual candles. This task is dangerous because looking directly at ritual elements or staying near them for too long accelerates Sanity loss.

Efficient teams split up to cover more ground, as candles can spawn in treatment rooms, the lobby, or even the storage areas. Following a set routine ensures no candle is missed before the Sanity meter hits the red zone.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Ritual Zone">
    When the screen begins to dim or turn a deep shade of red, stop your current treatment task. The ritual has a "zone of influence" where Sanity drains faster. Move toward the center of the disturbance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Glowing Candles">
    Scan the floor and corners of the immediate area. Ritual candles typically glow with a purple or dark red flame. There are usually 3 to 5 candles that must be extinguished to end the event.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Interact to Extinguish">
    Approach each candle and use the interaction key. Be aware that your Sanity will take a sharp hit the moment you extinguish a candle. This is the "sacrifice" mechanic of the ritual.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Recover Sanity Immediately">
    Once the final candle is cleared and the environment returns to normal, consume Coffee or food. If you are playing as a Surgeon or Doctor, treat a patient immediately to trigger your passive Sanity recovery.
  </Step>
</Steps>
