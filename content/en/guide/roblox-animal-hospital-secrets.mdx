<Section icon={Shield} title="Sanity and Item Management Guide">

Your Sanity meter is your life bar. Once it hits zero, you are effectively out of the game. Many **roblox animal hospital secrets** involve knowing how to mitigate sanity loss during chaotic events like the Death Ritual or Wall Banger attacks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor the Meter">
    Keep an eye on the Sanity UI. It drops when you look at cursed photos, miss anomalies, or take damage from environmental threats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Use Recovery Items">
    Coffee and Food are your primary recovery tools. Do not waste them early; save them for the "Receptionist" who is constantly looking at suspicious patients.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip Defensive Tools">
    As shifts progress, buy a Taser or Fire Extinguisher from the shop. These items allow you to handle emergencies without losing massive amounts of sanity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Coordinate with the Team">
    If your sanity is low, swap roles. Move to a quiet treatment room while a high-sanity teammate takes over the front desk.
  </Step>
</Steps>
