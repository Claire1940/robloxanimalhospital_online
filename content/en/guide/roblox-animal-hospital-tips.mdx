<Section icon={Shield} title="Sanity and Survival Mechanics">

Sanity is your health bar in this game. If it hits zero, you die. Managing this meter is the difference between a successful 2026 run and a failed shift. Sanity drops when you look at anomalies, encounter "Wall Bangers," or fail to treat patients correctly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor the Meter">
    Check your Sanity level frequently. If the screen begins to distort or turn gray, you are in the danger zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Use Recovery Items">
    Coffee is your best friend. Use it after checking cursed photos or dealing with Skinwalkers. Do not waste it during the easy early-shift minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Avoid Sanity Drains">
    Don't stare at the Bed Monster or ceiling threats. Perform your task and look away immediately to minimize exposure.
  </Step>
</Steps>
