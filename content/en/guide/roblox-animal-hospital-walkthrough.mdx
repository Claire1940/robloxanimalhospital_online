<Section icon={Map} title="Roblox Animal Hospital Walkthrough: Core Gameplay Loop">

Working the night shift in [Animal Hospital (Anomaly)](https://www.roblox.com/games/78515283254292/Animal-Hospital) is a tense balancing act between medical duty and survival horror. As an employee of the clinic, your primary task is to process incoming animal patients at the reception desk, identify anomalies, and treat the healthy animals in designated rooms.

**Video Highlights:**
- **Reception Duties**: Checking IDs and visual cues at the front desk.
- **Anomaly Detection**: Using the shutters to turn away suspicious patients.
- **Treatment Execution**: Completing minigames in treatment rooms.

The flow of each shift relies on division of labor and quick decision-making. Admitting a single anomaly can unleash a hostile entity into the hospital, while treating a healthy patient incorrectly will result in their death and a severe penalty to your score.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Shift Survival Guide">

To survive your shifts consistently, you must follow a strict operational protocol. Use this step-by-step walkthrough to establish an efficient routine for your team.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Secure the Front Desk">
    As soon as the shift begins, at least one player must man the reception desk. This is the first line of defense. When a patient approaches, do not press the accept button immediately. Instead, look closely at their physical features.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Perform the Triple-Check Verification">
    Run a complete diagnostic check on the visitor. First, check their physical body for visual oddities. Second, compare their appearance to their official photo ID on the desk. Third, switch on the lobby CCTV camera to look for hidden distortions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Filter or Admit the Patient">
    If the patient passes all checks, admit them to the clinic. If you detect any anomaly, immediately hit the shutter button to lock them out. This safely rejects the entity and protects the clinic's internal sanity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Target Medical Treatments">
    Once a healthy patient is assigned to a room, head to their location. Check the medical computer to identify the required treatment, complete the room-specific minigame, and administer the exact item needed from the supply hallway.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Manage Sanity and Environmental Hazards">
    Between patient waves, monitor your Sanity meter. If a hazard like a Bed Monster or a fire occurs, pause treatments temporarily to clear the threat. Use Coffee to restore lost Sanity before it drops to zero.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Assign dedicated roles in multiplayer lobbies. Having one player permanently on reception and two players handling treatments and emergencies maximizes your survival rate.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="How to Spot and Reject Anomalies">

Anomalies are supernatural entities attempting to infiltrate the hospital. Identifying them requires keen observation, as their structural differences can be incredibly subtle or highly aggressive.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="In-Person Visual Clues">
    - **Extra or hollow eyes**
    - Realistic or razor-sharp teeth
    - Distorted grins and constant twitching
  </Card>
  <Card title="CCTV Camera Discrepancies">
    - **Black boxes covering face**
    - Elongated limbs or void-like bodies
    - Different features than seen at the desk
  </Card>
  <Card title="Photo ID Abnormalities">
    - **ID photo does not match patient**
    - Static or heavy grain on the image
    - Bloodshot eyes or cursed grins
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

If an anomaly bypasses your security checks, it will transform into a Skinwalker inside the clinic. Skinwalkers actively hunt players and attack patients. When this occurs, you must deploy defensive items like the X-Taser or firearms to neutralize the threat.

| Anomaly Type | Detection Method | Correct Reaction | Threat Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Visual Impostor** | Physical inspection / Photo ID mismatch | Pull desk shutters immediately | High |
| **Camera Phantom** | Check CCTV feed | Reject at front desk | Medium |
| **Bed Monster** | Look under treatment beds | Avoid looking directly / Clear room | High |
| **Wall Banger** | Listen for loud knocking near desk | Stay away from affected walls | Low |
| **Ceiling Lurker** | Look down / Avoid looking up | Keep camera tilted downward | Critical |

<Callout type="success" title="Detection Success">
Successfully rejecting an anomaly at the front desk rewards your team with bonus performance points and prevents sudden Sanity drops.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Items and Medical Treatment Reference">

Treating normal patients requires the correct application of medical tools. Using the wrong item can prove fatal to the patient and lower your shift score.

| Medical Item | Category | Primary Use Case | Common Mistake to Avoid |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Coffee** | Sanity | Restores lost Sanity during long runs | Drinking it early when Sanity is high |
| **X-Taser** | Defense | Disables active Skinwalkers and threats | Firing prematurely and wasting charges |
| **Fire Extinguisher** | Emergency | Puts out room and patient fires | Ignoring follow-up treatment after fire |
| **Ointment** | Treatment | Heals burn injuries on patients | Applying before extinguishing the fire |
| **Surgery Tools** | Treatment | Required for Room 8 surgical procedures | Leaving the room mid-surgery |
| **Eye Drops / Herbs** | Treatment | Standard early-game patient care | Mixing up drops with other liquids |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Treatment Rule">
Room 8 (Surgery), Room 7 (Heart Monitor), and Room 6 (X-Ray) feature complex interactive minigames. Never guess the treatment item; complete the screen sequence fully to reveal the correct cure.
</Callout>

</Section>
