<Section icon={Map} title="Core Gameplay Loop and Beginner Basics">

Working the night shift in a medical facility is stressful enough, but in the world of **Animal Hospital (Anomaly)**, the patients are just as dangerous as the diseases they carry. Success requires a disciplined approach to the "Observe, Verify, and Treat" cycle. Every patient that walks through the front door is a potential threat to your Sanity and the safety of the entire hospital.

New players often make the mistake of rushing patients into treatment rooms to earn cash quickly. However, the most important rule of this **roblox animal hospital survival guide** is that speed comes second to accuracy. Admitting a single anomaly can result in a Skinwalker outbreak that ends your run prematurely.

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: The Three-Point Check">
Always perform a visual check at the desk, a CCTV check for limb distortions, and a photo comparison before deciding to open the shutters. Never rely on just one source of information.
</Callout>

  <Step num="1" title="Initialize the Shift">

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Anomaly Detection and Threat Identification">

Anomalies are the primary antagonists of your shift. These entities mimic animals but possess distinct, often subtle, flaws in their appearance or behavior. Learning to spot these "tells" is the difference between a successful shift and a Sanity-draining disaster.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Some anomalies only reveal their true form on the CCTV cameras. A patient may look perfectly normal at the desk but show elongated limbs or a black void where their eyes should be on the monitor.
</Callout>

<Checklist
  id="anomaly-checklist"
  title="Visual Anomaly Red Flags:"
  items={[
    "Extra eyes or completely hollow eye sockets",

When you identify an anomaly, you must act decisively. Rejecting them prevents them from entering the facility and protects your Sanity. If an anomaly is accidentally admitted, it may transform into a **Skinwalker**. These hostiles will hunt players and patients alike, requiring the use of a Taser or Gun to neutralize.

| Anomaly Type | Detection Method | Best Counter | Risk Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Visual Mimic** | Front Desk View | Immediate Shutter | Medium |
| **CCTV Phantom** | Monitor Check | Shutter before entry | High |
| **Photo Glitch** | ID Comparison | Shutter before entry | Medium |
| **Skinwalker** | Audio/Visual Cues | Taser or Firearms | Critical |
| **Bed Monster** | Under-bed Check | Avoidance/Light | High |

<Section icon={Heart} title="Sanity Management and Survival Tactics">

In this **roblox animal hospital survival guide**, Sanity is your true health bar. If it hits zero, you die. Sanity is drained by making mistakes (admitting anomalies), witnessing horrific events (rituals), or staring too long at cursed objects.

To maintain high Sanity, players must utilize recovery items and class perks effectively. **Coffee** is the most reliable tool for recovery and should be purchased from the shop between shifts.

| Sanity Drainer | Impact | Recovery Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Missed Anomaly** | Heavy Loss | Coffee / Food |
| **Cursed Photo** | Gradual Loss | Stop looking / Coffee |
| **Skinwalker Attack** | Critical Loss | Taser + Recovery |
| **Death Rituals** | Constant Area Drain | Clear Candles / Leave Area |

<Callout type="success" title="Team Synergy Strategy">
In multiplayer, have one player dedicated to "Sanity Watch." This player carries extra Coffee and takes over the desk or treatment duties when another teammate's Sanity drops below 40%.
</Callout>

**Environmental Hazards:**
- **Wall Bangers**: Avoid interacting with them near the front desk unless prepared for a Sanity hit.
- **Ceiling Threats**: Never look directly up if you hear strange noises from the vents.
- **Green Slime**: Watch your step; slipping can cause minor damage and slow your movement during emergencies.

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Items and Medical Treatment Protocols">

The medical side of the hospital requires precision. Each room has a specific task that must be completed before the final treatment item can be applied. Failure to follow the correct sequence or using the wrong item will result in the patient's death.

| Treatment Room | Task Type | Required Tool/Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

- **Fire Extinguisher**: Necessary for spontaneous room fires and "Burning Patient" events.
- **Speed Cola**: Increases movement speed, allowing you to reach treatment rooms faster.
- **Ointment**: Used specifically for patients rescued from fires.
