<Section icon={Eye} title="Anomaly Detection and Identification">

The most important skill in **roblox animal hospital how to survive** is anomaly detection. Patients arrive at the front desk, and you must decide whether to treat them or reject them. There are three primary layers of inspection you should perform for every visitor.

### Visual Inspection Methods
1.  **Direct Observation**: Look at the patient's face and body. Look for extra eyes, distorted limbs, or unnatural grins.
2.  **CCTV Comparison**: Check the lobby and desk cameras. Some anomalies only reveal their true form on digital displays, showing black boxes over eyes or void-like bodies.
3.  **Photo Verification**: Compare the patient's appearance to their medical photo. If the photo is grainy, static-filled, or shows a different species, reject them.

| Anomaly Category | Common Signs | Recommended Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Physical** | Three eyes, sharp teeth, twitching, realistic eyes | Immediate Shutter |
| **Digital** | Stretched limbs on CCTV, black void body | Immediate Shutter |
| **Photographic** | Cursed photo, mismatched species, static effect | Immediate Shutter |
| **Behavioral** | Staring at camera, refusing to move, wall banging | Monitor and Shutter |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Sanity Management and Item Utility">

Your Sanity meter is your health bar. If it reaches zero, you die. Managing this resource is the difference between a failed run and a successful shift. Sanity is drained by seeing anomalies, failing rituals, or being attacked by Skinwalkers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor the Meter">
    Keep an eye on the Sanity bar at the bottom of your screen. If the screen starts to distort or turn gray, your Sanity is dangerously low.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Use Recovery Items">
    Coffee is your primary recovery tool. Drink it to restore a portion of your Sanity. Save it for late-shift emergencies or after clearing a ritual.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Avoid Cursed Visuals">

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Treatment Room Walkthrough">

Once a patient is admitted, they are sent to one of eight rooms. Knowing **roblox animal hospital how to survive** means knowing how to handle each room's specific mini-game.

1.  **Rooms 1-5 (Basic)**: Standard diagnostics. Check the computer, see the required medicine, and fetch it from the hallway.
2.  **Room 6 (X-Ray)**: You must complete a flashing square sequence. If you fail, the patient takes damage. Always wait for the sequence to finish before clicking.
3.  **Room 7 (Heart Monitor)**: Click the correct icons while avoiding the skulls. Skulls cause time penalties and patient health loss.
4.  **Room 8 (Surgery)**: The most dangerous room. You must use specific tools (Scissors, Scalpels) in the order shown on the screen. Leaving the room during surgery will kill the patient instantly.

| Room Type | Difficulty | Common Mistake | Survival Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rooms 1-5** | Easy | Bringing the wrong medicine | **Medium** |
| **Room 6** | Medium | Clicking squares too early | **Medium** |
| **Room 7** | Medium | Clicking the skull icons | **High** |
| **Room 8** | Hard | Leaving before completion | **Critical** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Treatment Tip">
Giving the wrong treatment item to a normal patient can kill them. This reduces your final shift score and prevents you from earning the maximum amount of Animal Coins. Double-check the computer screen before applying any medicine.
</Callout>
